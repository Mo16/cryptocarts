Overview

Crypto Cartz is an NFT project aimed at building The Supermarket - an online novelty snack store utilizing the branding power of your favorite NFT collections. Owning a Crypto Cart gives you membership into our community of Shoppers, and VIP access to The Supermarket:

• Gain access to premium snack collectibles not available to the public
• License one of your eligible NFTs to earn royalties, and a chance to be featured on an actual, IRL snack product

Licensing & Profit Share

Shoppers will be able to license their NFTs, adding them to our pool of usable intellectual property (IP). As we design products for The Supermarket, we’ll utilize the IP pool to build fun, collectible, snacks & drinks. Profits from The Supermarket will be shared with the community, including:

• Crypto Cartz holders
• Holders of NFTs licensed to the IP pool
• Holders of NFTs chosen from the pool to be featured on a product
